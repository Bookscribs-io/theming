movie: The Conquest of the Pole (1912)
======================================

:: Title
The Conquest of the Pole

:: Description
A film loosely inspired by contemporary events and by Jules Verne's Voyages Extraordinaires, follows the comic misadventures of an international group of explorers on an expedition to the North Pole, where they encounter a man-eating frost giant and a dangerous magnetic needle. 

:: Date
1912-05-03

:: Genre
sci-fi

:: Major Themes
passion for exploration [an international group of explorers go on an expedition to the North Pole],
airliner [an international group of explorers built a passenger airplane to fly to the North Pole],
women's suffrage [Suffragette interrupt the international group of male explorers and demand to participate in the expedition to the North Pole],
man vs. nature [international group of explorers at the North Pole],
man vs. beast [international group of explorers versus the giant frost monster],
