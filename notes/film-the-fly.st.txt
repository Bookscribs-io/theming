movie: The Fly (1958)
=====================

:: Title
The Fly

:: Description
A scientist who is transformed into a grotesque creature after a common house fly enters unseen into a
molecular transporter he is experimenting with, resulting in his atoms being combined with those of the insect,
producing a human-fly hybrid.

:: Date
1958-07-16

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fly_(1958_film)

species transmutation [André was integrated with a fly when both he and it went through a matter teleporter.],
