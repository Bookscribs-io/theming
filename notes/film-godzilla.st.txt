===============================================

:: Title
Ghidorah, the Three-Headed Monster

:: Description
The fifth film in the Godzilla franchise and Shōwa series and was the second
Godzilla film produced that year; production began following Mothra vs.
Godzilla. This film also marks the first appearance of King Ghidorah, a
recurring antagonist of the Godzilla franchise. The film was released in Japan
on December 20, 1964 and was given a theatrical release in the United States
in September 1965 by Continental Distributing as Ghidrah, the Three-Headed
Monster.
