movie: RoboCop (1987)
=====================

:: Title
RoboCop

:: Description
RoboCop centers on police officer Alex Murphy who is murdered by a gang of criminals and subsequently revived by the megacorporation Omni Consumer Products (OCP) as a superhuman cyborg law enforcer known as RoboCop.

:: Date
1987-07-17

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RoboCop

:: Ratings

:: Genre
sci-fi

gentrification [OCP corporation planned to replace Old Detroit with Delta City, ],

dystopia [Future Detroit was a dangerous, crime ridden slum.],
personal identity [To what extent was RoboCop still Murphy?],
multinational corporations in society [Megacorporation Omni Consumer Products (OCP) was running Detroit into the ground for money.],
law and order issue [We are made to think about what to do when a city is plagued with crime and how it became that way in the first place.],
life in the corporate world [We were given a view into the inner working of the megacorporation Omni Consumer Products.],
