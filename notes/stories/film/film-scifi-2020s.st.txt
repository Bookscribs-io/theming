Bloodshot

The story follows a Marine who was killed in action, only to be brought back to life with superpowers by an organization that wants to use him as a weapon. It is intended to be the first installment in a series of films set within a Valiant Comics shared cinematic universe.

2020-03-13

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bloodshot_(film)
