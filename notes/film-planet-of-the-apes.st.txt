movie: Planet of the Apes (1968)
================================

:: Title
Planet of the Apes

:: Description
An astronaut crew who crash-lands on a strange planet in the distant future. Although the planet appears desolate 
at first, the surviving crew members stumble upon a society in which apes have evolved into creatures with 
human-like intelligence and speech. The apes have assumed the role of the dominant species and humans are mute 
creatures wearing animal skins.

:: Date
1968-02-08

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planet_of_the_Apes_(1968_film)

:: Genre
sci-fi

:: Major Themes
nuclear holocaust [Chimps, gorillas, and orangutans succeeded Humans as the dominant creatures on the Earth after Humans annihilated themselves in a nuclear war.],
simian alien [Chimps, gorillas, and orangutans all evolved to be intelligent ans shared power on a planet that was thought by Human astronauts to be a world distant from Earth.],
creationism in society [A talking Human according to simian theology and the religious authorities tried to suppress knowledge of Taylor and Cornelius' theory that apes evolved from people.],
faith vs. reason [Zira and Cornelius were prosecuted as heretics for their view on simian evolution. Dr. Zaius insisted that there was no conflict between faith and science.],
persecution of heretics in society [Zira and Cornelius were indited as heretics for their view on simian evolution.],

:: Minor Themes
time travel [Astronauts traveled into Earth's distant future.],
interstellar space travel [Astronauts believed they had traveled to a planet in a distant solar system where apes were the dominant species, but they had actually traveled into Earth's distant future.],
multiple alien species evolved on the same planet [Chimps, gorillas, and orangutans all evolved to be intelligent ans shared power.],
hominid evolution [Chimps, gorillas, and orangutans evolved to become intelligent. Cornelius had a theory that Apes evolved from Humans.],
passion for space exploration [Astronauts were on a pioneering voyage to test Hasslein's theory of time dilation.],
animal testing [The chimp doctors were performing experimental brain surgery on humans to advance simian science.],
romantic love [Zira and Cornelius.],
fiancée and fiancée [Zira and Cornelius.],

:: Other Keywords
interspecies kiss [Taylor kissed Zira on the lips goodbye.],

:: Collections
Collection: Science fiction films of the 1960s


movie: Beneath the Planet of the Apes (1970)
============================================

:: Title
Beneath the Planet of the Apes

:: Description
In this sequel to the original, another spacecraft crashes on the planet ruled by apes, carrying astronaut Brent who searches for Taylor and discovers an underground city inhabited by mutated humans with psychic powers.

:: Date
1970-05-26

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beneath_the_Planet_of_the_Apes

:: Genre
sci-fi,

:: Major Themes
nuclear holocaust [It was revealed that the people of the 20th century had annihilated themselves in a nuclear conflagration. Taylor blew up the whole world but detonating a doomsday nuclear bomb at the films conclusion.],
telepathic alien [The mutants used telepathy to communicate and also project illusions, like walls of flames, into other's minds.],
subterranean civilization [The mutants had an underground civilization and worshiped an atomic bomb.],
war [The gorillas invaded the Forbidden Zone where the mutants lived and made war with them.], 
organized religion [The mutants worshiped an atomic bomb. Religion was also integral to the apes way of life.],
hatred among nations [The mutants thought the apes were nothing but evil and vice versa.],

:: Minor Themes
time travel [Brent and his crew traveled into the future to the Planet of the Apes.],
social activism [The Chimpanzees held a demonstration to protest the gorillas making war on the Forbidden Zone.],

:: Other Keywords
mutant, atomic bomb,
