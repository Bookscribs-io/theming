movie: Before I Hang (1940)
===========================

:: Title
Before I Hang

:: Description
Dr. John Garth is on trial for murder after performing a mercy killing on an elderly friend. In the trial, he reveals that he had been researching a cure for aging, but had not had time to perfect it before his friend's pain became unbearable. Despite his pleas for mercy, the judge sentences him to be hanged in three weeks' time.

:: Date
1940-09-17

:: Genre
sci-fi

:: Major Themes
the quest for immortality [Dr. John Garth uses anti-aging serum on himself and encourages others to use it to prolong their lives.],
anti-aging technology [Dr. John Garth develops an anti-aging serum from the blood of an executed prisoner and uses it on himself.],
coping with aging [Dr. John Garth developed an anti-aging serum to because he felt aging was an unnecessary evil.],
the need for death [Dr. John Garth contended that death was undesirable and unnecessary, but others argued that death was natural and ought to be accepted.],
father and daughter [Martha Garth worried over her father John Garth's impending execution and then again after he took anti-aging serum.],

:: Minor Themes
facing one's own execution [Dr. John Garth is sentenced him to be hanged in three weeks' time.],
scientific experimentation [Dr. John Garth in the prison laboratory developing anti-aging serum.],


movie: The Ape (1940)
=====================

:: Title
The Ape

:: Description
A kindly scientist seeks to cure a young woman's polio. Meanwhile, a vicious circus ape has broken out of its cage and is terrorizing the townspeople.

:: Date
1940-09-30

:: Genre
sci-fi

:: Major Themes
coping with a disability [Miss Frances Clifford had polio and was confined to a wheelchair.],
romantic love [Frances Clifford and Danny Foster.],
paralysis reversing technology [Dr. Bernard Adrian developed a polio paralysis reversing serum from the spinal fluid of dead men.],

:: Minor Themes
scientific experimentation [Dr. Bernard Adrian in his laboratory developing cure for polio.],
unethical human experimentation [Dr. Bernard Adrian was criticized for performing unethical experiments on dogs and humans.],
